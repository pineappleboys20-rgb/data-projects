# Gold vs Silver Price Analysis (2006-2026)

## Overview
Analysis of 20 years of gold and silver price data to identify 
trends, correlations, and seasonal patterns using Python and Pandas.

## Questions Answered
1. What was the highest and lowest gold price ever recorded?
2. How did gold perform year by year?
3. Is there a correlation between gold and silver prices?
4. Which months are historically best and worst for gold?

## Key Findings
 Gold has had a massive growth throughout the years almost 8x since 2006
Gold and silver has a strong correlation 
Best month to buy is June since it's historically the cheapest by then
Best month to sell is Febraury since it's historically the highest by then
Driven by crisis Major spikes happen during crisis where when people lose trust on the economye

## Tools Used
- Python 3
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

## Dataset
- Source: [Kaggle — 20 Years of Gold vs Silver Prices](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aiwithcagri/20-years-of-gold-vs-silver-prices-ratio-daily-data)
- Timeframe: 2006-2026
- Records: 5,030 daily entries
